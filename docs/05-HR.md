# MODULE: Human Resources (HR)

## Overview
The HR module manages the complete employee lifecycle: hiring, onboarding, attendance, leave, performance, and offboarding. It is tightly coupled with the Payroll module for compensation management.

---

## 1. Employee Records

### 1.1 Personal Information
- Full name, preferred name, date of birth, gender, nationality
- National ID / passport number (encrypted at rest)
- Home address, emergency contacts (name, relationship, phone)
- Profile photo
- Personal email and personal phone (separate from work contact)

### 1.2 Employment Details
- Employee ID (auto-generated)
- Employment type: full-time, part-time, contract, intern, probationary
- Start date, probation end date, contract end date (if applicable)
- Department, sub-department
- Job title, job grade/band
- Work location (branch/warehouse)
- Direct manager (forms org chart)
- Employment status: active, on leave, suspended, terminated

### 1.3 Compensation (Linked to Payroll)
- Base salary or hourly rate
- Currency
- Pay frequency: monthly, bi-monthly, bi-weekly, weekly
- Salary history (with effective dates — never deleted)
- Bonus eligibility and target

### 1.4 Work Contact
- Work email (auto-generated: firstname.lastname@company.com)
- Work phone/extension
- Work address (if different from company default)

### 1.5 Documents
- Upload and store: employment contract, ID copy, qualification certificates, NDA
- Document types and retention periods configurable
- Document expiry alerts (work permits, professional licenses, medical certificates)
- E-signature support for employment contracts

### 1.6 Custom Fields
- Tenant-defined custom fields (e.g., "Driver's License Number", "Professional Membership ID")
- Field types: text, number, date, dropdown, yes/no
- Custom fields can be marked as required and/or sensitive (restricted access)

---

## 2. Organizational Structure

### 2.1 Departments
- Department hierarchy (Department → Sub-department)
- Department head assignment
- Department cost center (linked to Accounting)
- Headcount budget per department

### 2.2 Positions / Job Roles
- Position library (e.g., "Senior Sales Associate", "Warehouse Picker")
- Each position has: job description, required qualifications, pay grade range
- Positions can be filled by one or more employees
- Vacancy tracking: open positions not yet filled

### 2.3 Org Chart
- Auto-generated from manager-employee relationships
- Interactive tree view: click to expand departments
- Print/export as image or PDF
- Show/hide contractors, show by department or reporting line

### 2.4 Headcount Reporting
- Current headcount by department, location, employment type
- Headcount vs budget
- New hires in last 30/60/90 days
- Planned headcount changes (upcoming start dates, contract ends)

---

## 3. Recruitment

### 3.1 Job Requisition
- Department head creates job requisition (linked to open position)
- Requisition details: position, number of openings, urgency, budget
- Approval workflow (HR manager → Finance for budget confirmation)

### 3.2 Job Posting
- Create job posting from requisition
- Post to internal career portal
- Export to external job boards (LinkedIn, Indeed — manual posting or API)
- Posting status: draft, published, closed

### 3.3 Applicant Tracking
- Applicant profile: name, email, phone, resume/CV
- Application stages: applied → screening → interview → assessment → offer → hired/rejected
- Kanban board view of candidates per stage
- Stage notes per candidate per interviewer
- Email templates for each stage transition (acknowledgment, invite, rejection)

### 3.4 Interview Management
- Schedule interviews (date, time, interviewers, format: in-person/video/phone)
- Interview feedback form per interviewer
- Aggregate score across interviewers
- Offer letter generation from template (pre-filled with candidate and role details)
- E-signature on offer letter

### 3.5 Onboarding Checklist
- Onboarding task list assigned to: HR, IT, manager, new hire
- Tasks: provide laptop, set up email, ID badge, first-day schedule, training enrollment
- New hire self-service tasks: complete personal info, upload documents, e-sign policies
- Completion tracking with deadline alerts

---

## 4. Attendance & Time Tracking

### 4.1 Clock In/Out Methods
- Web app (browser-based)
- Mobile app (with optional GPS location capture)
- PIN entry on shared tablet at entrance
- Biometric device integration (fingerprint, face — via API/webhook)
- Manager manual entry for corrections

### 4.2 Work Schedules
- Define work schedule templates: regular (Mon–Fri, 9–5), rotating shifts, 24/7 schedules
- Assign schedule to employee or department
- Multiple schedule types per employee (e.g., half day Fridays)
- Public holiday calendar (per country, per location)

### 4.3 Overtime Rules
- Overtime threshold per day (e.g., >8 hours) and per week (e.g., >40 hours)
- Overtime rate multiplier (1.25x, 1.5x, 2x for public holidays)
- Overtime requires pre-approval or is automatic based on clock records
- Overtime cap per week/month

### 4.4 Timesheet Management
- Employees submit weekly/bi-weekly timesheets
- Timesheet: daily hours, project/cost center allocation (optional)
- Manager review and approval
- Rejected timesheets returned with comments
- Approved timesheets flow to Payroll automatically

### 4.5 Attendance Anomalies
- Late arrival / early departure flags
- Missing clock-out (trigger reminder + manager flag)
- Consecutive absent days alert
- Monthly attendance summary per employee

---

## 5. Leave Management

### 5.1 Leave Types
- Annual leave (vacation), sick leave, maternity/paternity, unpaid leave, compassionate leave, study leave, public duty leave
- Tenant can add custom leave types
- Per leave type: paid/unpaid, requires approval, maximum consecutive days, carry-over rules

### 5.2 Leave Balances & Accruals
- Opening balance on hire date (or January 1 per policy)
- Monthly accrual (e.g., 1.5 days per month for annual leave)
- Pro-rated for partial month joiners/leavers
- Maximum accrual balance (excess forfeited or paid out per policy)
- Carry-over to next year (maximum carry-over, expiry date for carried-over days)

### 5.3 Leave Request Workflow
- Employee submits leave request: type, start date, end date, notes
- Half-day option
- Attachment for sick leave (medical certificate)
- Manager receives notification to approve/reject
- HR can see and manage all leave requests
- Approved leave blocks calendar and deducts balance in real-time

### 5.4 Leave Calendar
- Team calendar view: see who is on leave on any day
- Filter by department
- Holiday calendar overlaid
- Prevent approval if minimum team coverage violated (configurable rule)

### 5.5 Leave Reports
- Leave balance report: current balances for all employees
- Leave utilization: leave taken vs entitled
- Leave liability: value of untaken leave (for financial provisions)
- Absenteeism report: sick days trend

## 6. Performance Management

### 6.1 Goal Setting (OKRs / KPIs)
- Company-level objectives cascaded to department and individual
- Employee sets personal goals (approved by manager)
- Goals: title, description, target, metric, deadline, weight
- Progress updates by employee (weekly/monthly)
- Goal status: not started, in progress, at risk, achieved, missed

### 6.2 Performance Reviews
- Review cycles: annual, bi-annual, quarterly (configurable)
- Review types: self-assessment, manager assessment, 360° (peers + reports)
- Rating scale: 1–5, 1–10, or text-based (below expectations, meets, exceeds)
- Structured review form: goals performance, competencies, development feedback
- Calibration: HR can compare ratings across managers to normalize

### 6.3 360° Feedback
- Request feedback from: peers, direct reports, cross-functional colleagues
- Anonymous option (identity hidden from reviewee)
- Aggregated feedback report with numerical and qualitative summary

### 6.4 Development Plans
- Post-review development goals
- Training recommendations linked to skills gaps
- Action items with owner and deadline
- Track progress on development goals

### 6.5 Performance Improvement Plan (PIP)
- Formal PIP document: concerns, expected improvements, timeline, support offered
- Manager and employee e-sign PIP
- Regular check-in schedule
- Outcome: closed successfully, extended, or escalated to disciplinary action

---

## 7. Disciplinary & Grievance

### 7.1 Disciplinary Process
- Incident recording: date, description, witnesses, evidence upload
- Warning types: verbal warning, written warning, final written warning, suspension, termination
- Each step documented with e-signature from manager and employee
- HR review and approval before formal warnings are issued
- Legal hold: disciplinary records retained per jurisdiction requirements

### 7.2 Grievance Management
- Employee submits grievance (anonymously or named)
- Assigned to HR investigator
- Investigation steps logged
- Resolution and outcome recorded
- Appeals process

---

## 8. Offboarding

### 8.1 Resignation & Termination
- Resignation: employee submits notice, manager acknowledges, last working day set
- Termination: HR initiates, reason and documentation required
- Exit interview: structured questionnaire
- Final settlement calculation (outstanding leave payout, notice period, severance)

### 8.2 Offboarding Checklist
- Tasks assigned to: IT (revoke access, collect devices), HR (final documents, reference letter), Finance (final payroll), manager (handover)
- Completion tracking with deadline

### 8.3 Access Revocation
- Trigger to IT: revoke all system access on last working day
- Integration hook: deactivate user in ERP, trigger notification to IT team
- Asset return tracking: company laptop, phone, access card

---

## 9. HR Self-Service Portal

- Employee can: view/edit personal info, view payslips, apply for leave, view leave balance, view company policies, submit expense claims, update emergency contacts
- Manager can: view team leave calendar, approve/reject requests, view team timesheets, access team member profiles
- Role-based access: employees see only their own data; managers see their direct reports' data

---

## 10. Compliance & Reporting

### 10.1 Statutory Reports
- Headcount and turnover report
- Leave liability report (for financial statements)
- Overtime hours report
- Contract expiry report
- Work permit expiry report

### 10.2 HR Analytics
- Time-to-hire (days from job posting to offer acceptance)
- Cost-per-hire
- Turnover rate (voluntary, involuntary) by department
- Absenteeism rate
- Training hours per employee
- Performance rating distribution

---

## 11. Integrations

- **Payroll:** Approved timesheets, confirmed leave, salary changes all flow to Payroll automatically
- **Accounting:** Employee cost center assignment for payroll journal entries
- **Projects:** Billable hours from timesheets flow to project billing
- **IT Systems (via webhook):** New hire triggers account creation; offboarding triggers account revocation
- **Email/Calendar:** Interview invitations synced to Google/Outlook Calendar
